Step One: Identify and Prepare Your Planting Site

Location, location, location! This holds just as true for gardening as it does for real estate. Click the button above corresponding to the seeds you received to choose a site that'll best serve your plants' needs. Clear out any grass (preferably from the roots) to expose bare soil. Important: Soak your chosen area BEFORE planting to ensure there's plenty of water. And if you haven't already, fill out our interest form!

Step Two: Plant and Give Love (and H2O)

After the water has soaked into your planting site (but before it has dried up), plant your seeds by using a shovel, trowel, stick, etc. to dig a shallow trench. Again, refer to the buttons above for specific instructions on how deep to plant the seeds. Once planted, they'll need regular watering, everyday when they're first beginning to germinate and 2-3 days thereafter. Check out (insert link) for a dirt-simple auto watering system if you don't think you'll be able to water by hand reliably. You may also have to remove weeds that will compete for nutrients and possibly shade out your crops, especially in the early stages.

Step Three: Document and Harvest

After several weeks, if everything goes right you should have a bounty to harvest. Congrats! At this point, we'll ask you to take a photo of what you've grown and we'll coordinate the delivery of your tree in the fall. Don't worry if something goes wrong with your crops! As long as we see that you've at least tried, then we'll send you a tree. Gardening can be unpredictable!
